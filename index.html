Grocery list

Walmart / Aldi Cheap List

Protein:
- Chicken thighs
- Ground beef
- Eggs

Carbs:
- Rice
- Pasta
- Bread
- Tortillas
- Potatoes

Extras:
- Cheese
- Peanut butter
- Pasta sauce or BBQ sauce

Produce:
- Bananas
- Frozen vegetables

Total target: about $50 to $70

7-day meal plan

NO-BRAIN MEAL PLAN

Day 1
Lunch: Chicken and rice
Dinner: Beef pasta

Day 2
Lunch: Egg sandwich
Dinner: Chicken tacos

Day 3
Lunch: Leftover chicken and rice
Dinner: Pasta and sauce

Day 4
Lunch: Peanut butter sandwich and banana
Dinner: Ground beef and potatoes

Day 5
Lunch: Egg sandwich
Dinner: Chicken and frozen vegetables

Day 6
Lunch: Leftovers mix
Dinner: Tacos again

Day 7
Lunch: Whatever is left
Dinner: Finish remaining food

If you forgot groceries

IF YOU FORGOT GROCERIES

Cheap backup meals:
- Eggs and toast
- Peanut butter sandwich
- Rice and frozen vegetables
- Tortilla and cheese

Rule:
Always keep two backup meals at home.
