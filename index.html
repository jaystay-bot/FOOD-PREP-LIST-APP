    Module 2

    7-day meal plan

    Simple, repeatable, forgiving. Leftovers are built in.

    Day 1 Garlic Chicken & Rice

    Ingredients

    • 2 chicken thighs
    • 1 cup white rice (dry)
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • Olive oil
    • Salt & pepper

    Steps

    1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
    2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    3. Cook chicken 6–8 min per side until browned and cooked through.
    4. Cook rice separately per package directions.
    5. Serve chicken over rice.

    Day 2 Ground Beef Pasta

    Ingredients

    • 1/2 lb ground beef
    • 8 oz pasta
    • 1 cup pasta sauce
    • Olive oil
    • Salt & pepper

    Steps

    1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente; drain.
    2. Brown ground beef in a pan with salt and pepper.
    3. Drain fat, add pasta sauce, simmer 5 min.
    4. Toss pasta with the beef sauce.
    5. Serve hot.

    Day 3 Scrambled Egg Tortilla Wrap

    Ingredients

    • 3 eggs
    • 2 tortillas
    • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
    • Olive oil
    • Salt & pepper

    Steps

    1. Beat eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    2. Heat oil in a pan, scramble eggs over low-medium heat until just set.
    3. Warm tortillas in a dry pan, ~30 sec per side.
    4. Fill tortillas with eggs and cheese.
    5. Fold and serve.

    Day 4 Chicken Tacos

    Ingredients

    • 1 chicken thigh, diced
    • 3 tortillas
    • Shredded cheese
    • Olive oil
    • Salt & pepper

    Steps

    1. Dice chicken into small pieces.
    2. Heat oil in a pan, add chicken, season with salt and pepper.
    3. Cook 4–5 min until cooked through.
    4. Warm tortillas in a dry pan.
    5. Fill with chicken and cheese. Serve.

    Day 5 Peanut Butter & Banana Sandwich

    Ingredients

    • 2 slices bread
    • Peanut butter
    • 1 banana
    • (Optional) pinch of salt

    Steps

    1. Spread peanut butter generously on one slice of bread.
    2. Slice banana thinly.
    3. Lay banana slices on the peanut butter.
    4. Close the sandwich.
    5. Optional: toast in a dry pan 2 min per side for a warm version.

    Day 6 Beef & Roasted Potatoes

    Ingredients

    • 1/2 lb ground beef
    • 2 potatoes, diced
    • Olive oil
    • Salt & pepper

    Steps

    1. Toss diced potatoes with oil, salt, and pepper.
    2. Roast at 425°F for 25 min (or pan-fry 15 min, stirring).
    3. Brown ground beef in a pan with salt and pepper.
    4. Drain fat.
    5. Plate beef next to potatoes and serve.

    Day 7 Chicken & Frozen Vegetables

    Ingredients

    • 1 chicken thigh
    • 2 cups frozen vegetables
    • Olive oil
    • Salt & pepper

    Steps

    1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
    2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    3. Cook chicken 6–8 min per side until cooked through.
    4. Add frozen vegetables to the same pan; cook 5–7 min until tender.
    5. Plate and serve.
